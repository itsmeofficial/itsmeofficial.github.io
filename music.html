👑 🐐 NOH SALLEH 🐐 👑

G.O.A.T. Greatest of all Time. A label that i would definitely give to this man. Noh Bin Salleh. Malaysia music icon Noh Salleh is the charismatic frontman of the award-winning rock band Hujan. We get him to tell us the songs that have made a big impact on his life and his music.

Born and raised in Miri, Sarawak, Noh moved to Kuala Lumpur and work as an assistant in recording studio own by Ahmad Idham. In between music sessions, he self-taught musical knowledge and ask the musicians about jazz chords that interested him. He began his career with Hujan in 2005, along with guitarist AG Coco who became a dominant figure in independent music scene. Their self-titled debut album Hujan in 2008, became the fastest-selling debut album in Malaysia history with massive success from the single "Pagi yang Gelap" and "Bila Aku Sudah Tiada".
